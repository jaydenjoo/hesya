FAQ · 자주 묻는 질문

Questions a smart traveler asks.

Do salon staff actually speak English / Japanese / Chinese?

92% of K-Verified salons have at least one staff member fluent in English. Japanese, Chinese (simplified + traditional), and Vietnamese support is available via the in-app translator — auto-detects your language and routes messages live, both ways.

What if my AI style match says "not advised"?

We tell you why — hair texture, scalp condition, or technique mismatch — and suggest 3 alternative looks that work for your features. No surprises after you arrive.

How do payments work?

Pay in your local currency (JPY, CNY, USD, VND, etc.). We handle currency conversion and settle to the salon in KRW. No hidden fees — the price you see is the price you pay.

Can I cancel my booking?

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before. We auto-translate the cancellation message so the salon never gets confused about why you canceled.

Is it really safe for solo women?

All partner salons are verified for location (≤ 5 min from subway), staff composition (92% have female stylists on staff), and 24/7 human-moderated chat support is available in your language — not bots.

Do you have a salon I can recommend?

Yes — refer them at hesya.com/refer. We give you ₩50,000 credit when they're onboarded as a K-Verified partner.
